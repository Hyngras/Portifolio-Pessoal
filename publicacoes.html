Artigo: Automatic identification of preferred music genres: an exploratory machine learning approach to support personalized music therapy

This work proposes an architecture for a music recommendation system aimed at assisting music therapists in treating elderly individuals with early-stage dementia. Utilizing data from a public music database, different classification techniques were evaluated to recommend songs according to musical genre. The results showed that the most suitable architecture was the Random Forest, with high precision and promising performance indicators. This approach may pave the way for the development of personalized recommendation systems to support music therapy in dementia patients. Leia o artigo completo.
